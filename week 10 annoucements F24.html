November is known as Movember, an annual event to help raise awarness for men's health issues such as testicular cancer, prostate cancer and men's suicide. In support of Movember, we encourage all who can to start with a cleanly shaven face and grow out a moustache this month, as moustaches are the symbol for the mission of the Movember Foundation.

Throughout November, the Engineering Society will be putting on events in support of the Movember foundation as we compete with the Aggies to see who can raise the most money, our prize being to crash/take over an Aggie Pub night.

Keep an eye out for our fundraisers, and thank you for your generosity in helping us support an important cause.
